Be a Good Person

Act with integrity. Be worthy of trust.
Meet others with kindness. Remember
that who you are is more important
than what you do.

Get Things Done

Take initiative. Have a can-do-attitude.
Value results, not working hours.
Be efficient, but above all, be effective
- what you do and don't do are equally
important.

Give Back

Support. Colaborate. Value your community.
Don't ask what others can do for you,
ask what you can do for others.

Learn and Grow

Be curious. Keep learning.
Empbrace imperfection. Know that your
qualities aren't fixed - you can grow and
develop throughout life.

Think Big

Be bold. Trust yourself and your skills.
Take risks, but not blindly - do so with
entrepreneurial responsibility, ownership
and humility.
